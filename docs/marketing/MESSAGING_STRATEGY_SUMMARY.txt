7. TONE OF VOICE: GUIDELNES
================================================================================

BRAND TONE: Confident, Helpful, Human

✓ DO:
  • Use conversational language ("You", direct address)
  • Lead with benefits, not features
  • Show empathy for pain points
  • Use specific numbers ("Save 12 min" not "Save time")
  • Celebrate user wins
  • Be human and authentic

✗ DON'T:
  • Sound corporate or robotic
  • Use technical jargon
  • Oversell or make unrealistic promises
  • Be condescending
  • Use vague language
  • Make it complicated

EJEMPLO DE MEJORA:
  Bad:  "Our platform utilizes machine learning to calculate risk"
  Good: "Know about rain 45 minutes before it arrives"
